Our Story

Built from a Personal Vision

Lim Health Service began in 2018 when Dr. Nathan Lim witnessed firsthand how difficult it was for patients in underserved communities to book timely appointments with the right specialists. Armed with a mission and a team of engineers and physicians, he launched what would become the region's most innovative medical platform.

Today, Lim Health Service operates across 6 cities, serves over 12,000 patients annually, and partners with 48+ board-certified specialists spanning 14 medical disciplines.
